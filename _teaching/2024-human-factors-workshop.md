Award-winning "gamified" Human Factors workshop for Year 5 medical students, embedded into the 9-week "Acute and Critical Care" block at the South Bristol Academy for the 2025/26 academic year.

Uses the co-operative puzzle game "Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes" to teach non-technical skills — communication, teamwork, situational awareness, and leadership under pressure — in a non-clinical context.

**Recognition:**
* Finalist: Teaching Innovation and Excellence (TIE) Prize — ASME, 2025
* National Oral Abstract — DEMEC, 2025
